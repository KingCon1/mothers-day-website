A Special Tribute to an Amazing Mum

1A Thoughtful Creation

🎨

I created this Mother's Day slideshow as a special way to show my appreciation for my mum. I wanted to make something thoughtful and unique just for her, something that would make the day feel a little more special.

2Learning & Growing

📚

This project was challenging for me because I had to learn new things and spend time designing and organising the slides. Even though it was difficult at times, I kept working on it because my mum means a lot to me and I wanted to do something meaningful for her.

3A Labor of Love

❤️

I've never made something this complex before, but that's what makes it important to me. It represents the effort I put in and the care I have for my mum. I hope that when she sees it, she knows how much I appreciate everything she does for me.
